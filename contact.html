// HOW TO REACH US

Where Goodway delivers — and how fast we respond.

We are based in Abu Dhabi and ship across all seven emirates. Whether you are preparing a BOQ for a new facility, running urgent spare-parts sourcing for a live oilfield, or specifying Civil-Defence-approved materials for a building handover, the channels below are the fastest way to get a VAT-compliant quotation.

Service area

All seven emirates: Abu Dhabi, Dubai, Sharjah, Ajman, Umm Al Quwain, Ras Al Khaimah, and Fujairah. Site delivery is arranged case-by-case for offshore platforms and remote operator locations. For GCC-wide export enquiries, please contact us for specific terms.

Response times

Enquiries received before 3:00 PM GST get a same-day acknowledgement. A formal quotation with lead time is issued within one business day for in-stock items, up to 2–3 business days for complex BOQs requiring factory-lead-time confirmation from our principals.

Urgent / after-hours

For time-critical oil & gas field supply and emergency lighting or safety requirements, reach our mobile line directly on +971 56 442 3539 or via WhatsApp. Outside of standard hours we triage urgent enquiries and get back with a scheduled supply plan by the next business morning.

What to prepare

To get a same-day quote, send us the part number / manufacturer / spec sheet / BOQ line item, the delivery emirate, required-by date, and whether the project needs specific compliance paperwork (Civil Defence, NFPA 80, Free Sale, Certificate of Conformity). We can also work from photographs of a replacement part.

Office hours

Monday to Saturday, standard UAE business hours. Closed on Friday for the weekly rest day. Closed on public holidays declared by the UAE government (Eid Al Fitr, Eid Al Adha, National Day, Commemoration Day, New Year, Islamic New Year, Prophet Mohammed's Birthday).

Who to ask for what

Sales & quotations — email gwayuae@outlook.com or call +971 2 245 0497. After-sales / warranty — same channels; our operations team will route your claim. Supplier / principal partnership — mention "partnership" in the subject line and we'll forward to management.
