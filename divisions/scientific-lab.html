// Frequently asked

Questions we hear most about Scientific & Lab Instrumentation.

Do lab instruments come with calibration certificates?
Yes — every calibration-grade instrument (analytical balances, spectrophotometers, pH meters, digital hydrometers) ships with a traceable certificate from the principal, ISO 17025-referenced where applicable.
How do you handle temperature-sensitive reagents in UAE transit?
Merck and Fisher Scientific cold-chain items ship refrigerated via our UAE freight partners with temperature loggers. We reject any delivery that breaks the chain and reorder.
Can you supply full laboratory fit-outs?
Yes — bench systems, fume hoods, analytical benches, ovens, centrifuges and consumables can be quoted as a single project package with installation coordination.
What is the typical lead time for Fisher Scientific items?
Catalogue items are typically 3–6 weeks ex-UK; specialist reagents can be 6–10 weeks depending on lot availability. Urgent chemistry is often air-freighted.
