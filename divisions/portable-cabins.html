// Frequently asked

Questions we hear most about portable cabins.

Do you sell cabins outright, or only rent?
Both. Outright purchase ships with a VAT-compliant invoice and the cabin becomes the buyer's asset. Monthly rental starts from 1 month, includes maintenance on longer terms, and the cabin is collected at the end of the rental.
What is the minimum rental period?
One month is the minimum. Most projects rent for 3–12 months. Long-term rates and deposits are confirmed on enquiry — share the project location, cabin type, quantity and required dates.
Are the cabins air-conditioned?
Yes — wall-mounted split A/C is supplied as standard on every cabin. Window-mounted A/C is available as a lower-cost alternative. The unit is sized to the cabin volume so the interior stays comfortable through UAE summer.
Can the cabins be customised?
Yes. Beyond the standard ablution, office, accommodation and guard configurations, we build to client drawing — bespoke widths, internal partitioning, multiple doors, livery and branding, integrated generator rooms, and so on. Custom builds typically take 2–4 weeks from order.
Do you handle delivery and craning?
Yes — delivery and crane offload across all seven emirates is part of the quote. We coordinate the trailer, crane and offload schedule with the project's site team. GCC-wide export is arranged on request.
Where is your yard?
