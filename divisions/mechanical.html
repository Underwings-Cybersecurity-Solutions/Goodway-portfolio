// Frequently asked

Questions we hear most about Mechanical Items.

Which compressor OEMs does Goodway support?
Over 55 compressor OEMs, including Atlas Copco, Ingersoll Rand, Sulzer, Cummins, Waukesha, Aerzen, Howden and Worthington. We source new spares, rebuild kits and obsolete parts with documented pedigree.
Do you supply ASME / ASTM certified valves and flanges?
Yes — all flanges, valves and pressure-rated fittings ship with ASME/ASTM material certificates and mill test reports where applicable.
Can you match obsolete compressor serial numbers?
Yes. Send the serial number and nameplate photo to Request a Quote; we trace the original OEM drawing and source matching or equivalent spares.
Do you supply lifting and lashing gear certified to LOLER?
Yes — slings, shackles, chain blocks and lashing kits are supplied with LOLER test certificates. We can arrange periodic inspection through our UAE partners.
