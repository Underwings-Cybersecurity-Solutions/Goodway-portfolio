// HOW WE WORK

From specification to site delivery — how Goodway handles every RFQ.

Whether you're an oil & gas operator ordering specialty chemicals, a main contractor spec'ing Civil-Defence-approved fire doors, or a government buyer sourcing motors and safety equipment — the process is the same, and it's designed to move faster than a typical UAE trading house.

01 · Enquiry & specification

Send us your BOQ, part number, or spec sheet.

Use the quote form, email info@goodway.ae, or call +971 2 245 0497. We accept partial specifications — our technical desk maps your requirement to the right principal, whether it's a Westinghouse AC motor, an AEES UPS system, or a Fisher Scientific lab instrument.

02 · Source & lead-time check

We confirm availability and factory lead time.

Some items ship from UAE stock; others are built-to-order at the principal's factory in France, Germany, USA, Japan or Australia. You get a clear lead-time window before committing, typically within one business day.

03 · VAT-compliant quotation

A formal quote with all compliance paperwork.

Goodway is VAT-registered (TRN 100464283900003) and operates under Abu Dhabi trade licence CN-1843054. Every quote is itemised, VAT-included, and accompanied by the relevant certificates of conformity, approval letters, and test reports your procurement team needs for audit.

04 · Delivery & after-sales

Freight, clearance, and site handover.

We handle inbound freight consolidation, UAE customs clearance, and warehouse-to-site delivery across all seven emirates. Warranty and after-sales support stay with Goodway as the authorised UAE channel — you deal with us, not the principal in Europe or the US.

Nine divisions, one supplier, zero vendor juggling.

Each division runs on its own authorised principal network, technical desk and compliance bundle — so you get specialist depth without managing nine vendor relationships, nine credit lines or nine sets of paperwork.

Send a mixed bill of quantities — lab instruments + motors + fire doors + stationery — and get one consolidated quotation with per-line principal, lead time and compliance paperwork already mapped. No re-routing between suppliers, no chased gaps.
