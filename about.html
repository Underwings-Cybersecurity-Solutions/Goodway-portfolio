// OUR APPROACH

Authorised distribution, not parallel imports.

Goodway operates as a direct channel between international principals and UAE buyers. Every product we ship is sourced through a formal distribution agreement — not a grey-market intermediary — which means documented origin, full manufacturer warranty, and accountability that stays in Abu Dhabi.

Original-equipment quality

Direct agreements with AEES, Lütze, Fisher Scientific, Merck, Yuasa, Westinghouse, ATMI, Efftec, McMaster-Carr, HERMA and Fraste. Every shipment carries the principal's serial numbers, certificates of conformity, and mill test reports where applicable.

Local warranty & after-sales

Warranty claims stay with Goodway as the authorised UAE channel. No international escalation, no courier returns to Europe, no translation delays — our technical desk handles the claim and coordinates with the principal on your behalf.

Compliance paperwork — done

Every quote includes the relevant paperwork bundle: Civil Defence approval letters, NFPA 80 test certificates, Certificates of Conformity, Free-Sale certificates, and manufacturer warranty terms. VAT-compliant invoicing under TRN 100464283900003.

Delivery across all 7 emirates

Warehouse-to-site delivery anywhere in Abu Dhabi, Dubai, Sharjah, Ajman, Umm Al Quwain, Ras Al Khaimah and Fujairah. Inbound freight consolidation, customs clearance and on-site handover managed end-to-end so your procurement team deals with one invoice and one accountable party.
