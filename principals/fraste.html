About Fraste

Italian hydraulic drilling expertise — from water wells to deep exploration.

Fraste was founded in 1962 by the Frasson brothers in Albaredo d'Adige, near Verona. Six decades on, the company exports its hydraulic drilling rigs to more than 80 countries — with a reputation for compact, manoeuvrable machines that punch above their weight on remote sites.

  • Water-well rigsFS & FSD series · up to 600 m depth
  • Geotechnical rigsMultidrill series · site investigation
  • Exploration / coringXL series · mineral & deep core sampling
  • Built in ItalyCE-marked · ISO 9001 · spares supported

Italian-built drilling rigs for water-well, geotechnical & exploration applications — engineered in Verona since 1962.

Fraste rig families on the Goodway catalogue.

Whether the project is a 200 m water-well drill in Al Ain or a geotechnical investigation in Mussafah, Goodway pairs the right Fraste rig family to the depth, formation and access constraints — with spares and operator support backed by the Italian factory.

01 · Water-well drilling

FS & FSD series
