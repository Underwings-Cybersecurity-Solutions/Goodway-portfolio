About HERMA

A century of self-adhesive technology, made in Germany.

HERMA started in 1906 making gummed labels and grew into one of Europe's leading manufacturers of self-adhesive products. Today the company runs three integrated divisions — self-adhesive material, labels and labelling machines — from its Filderstadt headquarters near Stuttgart.

  • Self-adhesive materialCoated face material in rolls for converters
  • Office & industrial labelsA4 sheets, address labels, durable industrial labels
  • Labelling machinesInline / roll-fed industrial labellers
  • German engineeringDIN-spec quality · documented origin

Self-adhesive material & paper rolls

Coated face material in rolls for converters and label printers — the raw stock that downstream presses turn into finished labels. Available in paper, polyester, polyethylene and polypropylene faces with a range of adhesives and liners.

  • Paper / PE / PP / PET face material
  • Permanent & removable adhesives
  • Glassine + PET liner options
  • Custom widths & roll diameters
  • Reach / FDA / RoHS compliant
  • Rolls cut to converter spec

Office, address & industrial labels

A4 sheet labels for office printing plus industrial-grade labels for asset, cable, pipe and packaging marking. The range covers everything from a 100-pack of mailing labels for a desk inkjet up to UV- and chemical-resistant tags for oilfield equipment.
